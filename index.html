// HOW IT WORKS

Four steps to clarity.

From your resume to a full gap analysis in under 30 seconds.

01 🕷️

Scrape

Pulls live job postings from Arbeitnow and RemoteOK for your target role and location.

02 📄

Parse

Extracts 150+ skills and your work experience timeline directly from your resume PDF.

03 📊

Analyze

Cross-references your skills against every job posting, ranking gaps by how often each one appears.

04 💡

Report

Generates a self-contained HTML report with charts, job table, and a personalized action plan.

// FEATURES

Everything you need.

Built for developers who are actively job searching.

📈

Skill Gap Ranking

Missing skills are ranked by how often they appear across all scraped jobs, so you know what to learn first.

💼

Experience Comparison

Compares your years of experience against what jobs actually require, with your full work history timeline.

🤖

AI Resume Tailoring

Uses Gemini AI (free) to rewrite your resume bullet points to better match a specific role — without inventing anything.

📋

Per-Job Breakdown

Every job in the report shows your exact match percentage with matched and missing skills highlighted.

🎯

Personalized Action Plan

Auto-generated recommendations with priority levels based on your specific gaps, not generic advice.

🔒

Fully Local

Your resume never leaves your machine. No accounts, no uploads, no tracking. The HTML report works offline.

// QUICK START

Up and running
in two minutes.

Requires Python 3.8+ and Node.js (for resume tailoring).

install
# Clone and install
git clone https://github.com/ZishuLin/job-skill-gap-analyzer.git
cd job-skill-gap-analyzer
pip install -r requirements.txt

# Run your first analysis
python main.py analyze -q "Software Engineer" -l "Canada" -r resume.pdf

# Tailor your resume with AI (free Gemini key required)
python main.py tailor -q "Python Developer" -r resume.pdf
